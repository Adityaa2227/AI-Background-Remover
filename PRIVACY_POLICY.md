# Privacy Policy for AI Background Remover

**Effective Date:** 2024-01-29

## 1. Introduction
AI Background Remover ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect
### 2.1 Image Data
- **Temporary Processing:** When you use our background removal service, the image you upload is sent to our server solely for processing.
- **No Permanent Storage:** We do not store, save, or share your images. Once the background is removed and the result is returned to you, the image is immediately deleted from our server's memory.

### 2.2 Device Information
- We may collect non-personal device information (e.g., model, OS version) to improve app stability and performance.

### 2.3 Advertising Data (Google AdMob)
- This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use:
    - Unique device identifiers (advertising ID)
    - Usage data and diagnostic information
    - Location data (if granted)
- This data is used to provide personalized ads and analytics.
- For more information, please review [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 3. Permissions
We may request the following permissions:
- **Storage/Photos:** To select images for processing and save the result.
- **Notifications:** To send you updates or processing status (using Firebase Cloud Messaging).
- **Internet:** To communicate with our background removal server and ad networks.

## 4. Third-Party Services
- **Firebase:** utilized for Push Notifications and Analytics.
- **Google AdMob:** utilized for advertising.

## 5. Security
We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data during transmission (HTTPS). However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
